About Me

We humans sometimes tend to do illogical mistakes and when we look back at it, we cannot reason to ourselves as to why we did those horrible mistakes. I guess that's why we are humans. Even I have done some terrible things in life and cannot reason as to why I have done at the first place. It doesn't sound logical at all. Similarly failures are by products of these mistakes. However the best lessons are also learnt from mistakes and failures. There is no better teacher than life itself.

My life story is nothing short of a movie. It has all the ingredients expect for action scenes. Oh.. there is tragedy, romance, drama and what not.. and just like movies there are miracles also. But in my case the movie isn't complete. I am yet to meet several characters, experience many things and have quite a few unfulfilled bucket list.
