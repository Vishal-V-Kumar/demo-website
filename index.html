I Am No Saint..!!

Not long ago, I didn't know where my life was heading and I was devastated. I had no confidence, didn't want to live either. But there was this voice with-in me that was trying to talk to me.. all I had to do was to listen to it.

|As Junot Diaz had once said.. But if these years have taught me anything it is this: you can never run away...The only way out is in.

One positive thought can be life changing. The first step is often the most difficult one. I took that first step towards positivity and it dramatically changed my life and with that I learnt.. what life is all about. I knew I wanted to make a difference, a difference with this new found life. I taught if I can do it, anybody can. Yes, each one of us have our own challenges, our own personal battles to fight. But we humans have unlimited potential to tap into. All we have to do is make that first move, sow that first seed of positivity and watch ourselves grow in confidence.
